Our commitment to DepEd’s vision

Gulayan sa Paaralan at Pablo Lorenzo NHS is a direct response to DepEd Memorandum No. 293, series of 2007 (revitalized under OUA Memo 00-0522-2022), which directs all schools to establish vegetable gardens as a strategy for:

food security nutrition & health hands-on agri-education
The school grounds shall be transformed into a sustainable vegetable garden — a “Gulayan sa Paaralan” — to promote health, climate resilience, and the DepEd core value of Maka-kalikasan.
— from DepEd memorandum (series 2022), adopted by Pablo Lorenzo NHS.
Read the full DepEd memorandum
Since 2023, we have harvested more than 200 kg of fresh vegetables used for our school feeding program.
